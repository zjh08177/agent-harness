# Level 3-1 Calibration Rubric

This reference contains the competency standards for level 3-1 engineers at ByteDance. Use this as the evaluation framework when writing Self-Review to ensure accomplishments align with calibration criteria.

---

## Level 3-1: A Technical Owner of Complex Products or Business

### Keywords
- Makes medium and long-term technical plans
- Oversees product development outcomes
- Supervises team building (echelon building and environment)
- Is influential at the department level
- Has foundational skills of working across platforms

---

## Dimension 1: Technical Competencies (技术能力)

| # | Criteria | How to Demonstrate |
|---|----------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Clearly understands the app's complete technical architecture**, making technical strategies for the medium and long term | Show architectural decisions, tech roadmaps, ERDs, long-term planning documents |
| 2 | **Has a broad technical view**, follows major technology trends, and is knowledgeable about the technical frameworks of related products | Reference industry trends, competitor analysis, cross-product knowledge |
| 3 | **Specializes in a mobile platform**, has explored its underlying platform, and has a preliminary understanding of other platforms | Deep iOS/Swift expertise + some cross-platform awareness (Android, Server) |
| 4 | **Leverages advanced experience** from both within and outside the company to enhance product technologies and business, expediting product development | Apply best practices, external learnings, novel approaches that accelerate delivery |

---

## Dimension 2: Business Competencies (业务能力 + 业务推动协调能力)

| # | Criteria | How to Demonstrate |
|---|----------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Has a clear idea of the product's overall business growth**, is aware of industry competitors' development trends, and offers personal insights and decisions | Show business awareness, competitive analysis, strategic input |
| 2 | **Engages in product planning**, manages and distributes resources appropriately, and assumes accountability for product results | Demonstrate resource allocation, project ownership, end-to-end accountability |
| 3 | **Leads or builds a technical team**, overseeing the development of a small to medium-sized app; conducts reasonable human resources planning and successfully develops a tiered team structure | Show team leadership, hiring/growth decisions, echelon building |

---

## Dimension 3: Team Contributions (团队贡献)

| # | Criteria | How to Demonstrate |
|---|----------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Oversees echelon building** and fosters individual growth within the team | Mentorship, career discussions, team member development |
| 2 | **Responsible for enhancing collective technical skills** by promoting knowledge sharing, setting technical standards, cultivating innovation, organizing tech-focused events | Knowledge sharing sessions, tech standards, code review culture |
| 3 | **Facilitates cross-functional sharing and training** | Cross-team presentations, training sessions, documentation |

## Self-Review Writing Strategy

When writing accomplishments, explicitly connect to calibration criteria:

**Template:**
```
[Accomplishment from Key Output] demonstrates [specific 3-1 criteria] by [concrete evidence/metrics].
```

**Example:**
```
Leading the AIGC Arch Tiger team to define the Universal AIGC Workflow Protocol demonstrates "clearly understanding the app's complete technical architecture and making technical strategies for the medium and long term" (Technical #1). The protocol now serves as the foundation for both client and server workflow managers, adopted across 7 major projects in Creation and Social verticals.
```

**For Areas of Improvement**, identify which 3-1 criteria need strengthening:
```
To strengthen "engaging in product planning and assuming accountability for product results" (Business #2), I plan to participate more actively in roadmap discussions and OKR setting for 2026.
```
