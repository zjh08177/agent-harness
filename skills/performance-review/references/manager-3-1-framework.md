## The 5 Evaluation Dimensions for 3-1

### 一、直接影响的交付与结果 (Product Ownership & Outcome)

**What the manager looks for:**
- 明确你直接负责、最终对结果负责的核心交付
- Clarify what you are **directly responsible for** and **accountable for the end result**

**Key elements to write clearly:**
- 直接负责交付解决了什么业务或产品问题
  - What business or product problem did YOUR direct delivery solve?
- 最终结果如何（指标、规模、稳定性、影响面）
  - What were the final results (metrics, scale, stability, impact scope)?

---

### 二、重点交付中的个人差异化 (Your Diff) ⭐ MOST CRITICAL

> **"这是 3-1 非常核心的判断点"** — This is THE CORE judgment point for 3-1

**The question to answer:**
- 在关键项目中，有你和没有你的差异是什么
- What's the difference between having you vs. not having you on key projects?

**What to include:**
- 关键技术判断或方案选择
  - Critical technical decisions or solution choices YOU made
- 在复杂局面下的推进与决策
  - How YOU drove progress and made decisions in complex situations
- 对产品方向、节奏或质量的实质性影响
  - YOUR substantive impact on product direction, pace, or quality

**Writing Template:**
```
Without my [specific decision/action], [what would have happened differently].
Because of my [specific contribution], [the outcome was X instead of Y].
```

---

### 三、复杂度与技术判断力 (Complexity & Technical Ownership)

**From a model perspective, the manager looks at whether you took on:**
- 中长期技术规划 (medium/long-term technical plans)
- 复杂产品或业务的技术 owner 角色 (technical owner role for complex product/business)

**Key points to emphasize:**
- 工作的复杂度、模糊度、不确定性
  - The complexity, ambiguity, and uncertainty of your work
- 你如何基于技术与业务判断做取舍
  - How YOU made trade-offs based on technical and business judgment
- 是否体现出对整体技术架构和业务演进的理解
  - Whether you demonstrate understanding of overall technical architecture and business evolution

---

### 四、整体方向感与交付质量 (Direction & Quality)

**Not just "did you finish the task" — also looks at:**
- 方向是否正确
  - Was the direction correct?
- 技术和产品方案是否成熟
  - Was the technical/product solution mature?
- 交付质量是否稳定、可复用、可持续
  - Was the delivery quality stable, reusable, sustainable?
- 这里对应的是你对产品整体业务增长、行业趋势和竞争态势的理解与判断
  - This corresponds to your understanding of overall product business growth, industry trends, and competitive landscape

---

### 五、团队与技术影响力 (Team & Org Impact)

**From 3-1 model, also looks at:**
- 是否在梯队建设和个人成长上承担责任
  - Taking responsibility for echelon building and individual growth
- 是否推动团队整体技术能力提升（技术规范、分享、创新氛围）
  - Driving team technical capability improvement (standards, sharing, innovation culture)
- 是否有跨团队/跨部门的技术分享或影响
  - Cross-team/cross-department technical sharing or influence

## Self-Review Writing Rules (Based on Manager Feedback)

### Rule 1: SEPARATE Direct vs Indirect Impact

**DO:**
```
[DIRECT IMPACT - I was accountable]
As the tech owner for AIGC offline processing, I:
- Made the decision to use X protocol over Y
- Drove the cross-team integration with Server/AME/Camera
- Directly accountable for the 100 QPS launch target

[INDIRECT IMPACT - I contributed/supported]
The AIGC publish rate improved from 0.04% to 0.53%. While many teams contributed to this metric, my direct contribution was the pipeline architecture that enabled 70% of new effect production.
```

**DON'T:**
```
Led cross-team integration that resulted in 13x publish rate improvement.
(This mixes direct action with indirect metric - unclear what YOU specifically caused)
```

### Rule 2: HIGHLIGHT Your Personal Differentiation

For every accomplishment, ask and answer:
1. What would have happened without me?
2. What critical decision did I make that others wouldn't have made?
3. What did I push for that changed the outcome?

**Template:**
```
In [project], I made the critical decision to [specific choice]. This was significant because [the alternative was X, but I chose Y because of Z]. As a result, [concrete outcome that wouldn't have happened otherwise].
```

### Rule 3: Show Technical Judgment, Not Just Execution

**Weak (just execution):**
```
Delivered the workflow manager integration for 7 projects.
```

**Strong (shows judgment):**
```
Advocated for protocol-first design over implementation-first approach. This required pushing back on initial timeline pressure, but resulted in 75% dev time reduction for subsequent integrations (8 days → 2 days per project).
```

- [ ] Have I demonstrated understanding of overall architecture and business evolution?
- [ ] Is my direction/quality judgment visible, not just "I finished it"?
- [ ] Have I shown team/org impact beyond personal achievements?
